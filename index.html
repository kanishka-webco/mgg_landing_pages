Your Installation Journey

Exactly What Happens —
Step by Step

We believe a great installation starts with great communication. Here's precisely what to expect from the moment you confirm through to post-installation support.

1

Deposit & Booking Secured

Once your 50% deposit is received, we lock in your installation date and begin sourcing your materials and coordinating your trade team for a smooth, efficient install.

2

Pre-Install Confirmation

A day before, you'll receive a confirmation message with everything you need to know — who's coming, what to expect, and how to prepare so the day runs smoothly.

3

Morning of — Live Tracking

On the morning of your installation, you'll receive a text notification with your technician's ETA and a live GPS tracking link. No waiting around wondering when they'll arrive.

4

Any Questions Before We Start?

Our team are always available by phone up to installation day. Have a question about the scope, access requirements, or what to move — just call us anytime.

1

Introduction & Walkthrough

Our lead technician will introduce themselves, walk you through exactly what's happening that day, and answer any questions before work begins. No surprises.

2

Multiple Trades Coordinated Seamlessly

Your job may require gas, electrical, and installation trades. We coordinate everything so team members pass through efficiently — you don't need to manage anyone.

3

Your Home is Treated with Respect

We lay drop sheets, protect surfaces, and clean up thoroughly when we're done. Any rubbish is removed from the site. We work to the highest professional standard.

4

System Tested & Signed Off

Before we leave, your new system is fully tested and commissioned in front of you. We'll show you how everything works and make sure you're completely happy.

1

Certifications Emailed Immediately

Once the job is complete and final payment received, we email you all trade certifications and compliance paperwork — your legal peace of mind, in writing.

2

Follow-Up Call from Our Team

A team member will call you a few days after installation to make sure everything is running perfectly and provide any ongoing support you need.

3

You're a Client for Life

We don't disappear after the job. Whether it's a question 2 weeks later or a service call 5 years later — our team is always available to support you as a valued client.

4

6-Year Workmanship Warranty

Every installation is backed by our 6-year workmanship warranty. If anything isn't right with our work, we'll fix it — no arguments, no charge.

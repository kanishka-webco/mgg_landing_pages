Exactly What Happens —
Step by Step

We believe a great installation starts with great communication. Here's precisely what to expect from the moment you confirm through to post-installation support.

1

Deposit & Booking Secured

Once your 50% deposit is received, we lock in your installation date and begin sourcing your equipment and coordinating your trade team.

2

Pre-Install Confirmation

A day before, you'll receive a confirmation message — who's coming, what to expect, and how to prepare so the day runs smoothly.

3

Morning of — Live Tracking

On the morning of your installation, you'll receive a text with your technician's ETA and a live GPS tracking link. No waiting around.

4

Any Questions? Just Call

Our team are always available by phone up to installation day. Questions about scope, access, or what to move — just call us anytime.

1

Introduction & Walkthrough

Our lead technician introduces themselves, walks you through what's happening that day, and answers any last questions before work begins.

2

Trades Coordinated Seamlessly

Gas, electrical, and installation trades coordinated by us. You don't manage anyone — we handle the whole show.

3

Home Treated with Respect

Drop sheets, surface protection, and full site clean-up on completion. All rubbish removed. We work to the highest professional standard.

4

System Tested & Signed Off

Your new system is fully tested and commissioned in front of you. We walk you through how everything works before we leave.

Certifications Emailed Immediately

2

Follow-Up Call

A team member calls you a few days after installation to make sure everything is running perfectly and answer any questions.

3

A Client for Life

We don't disappear after the job. Questions 2 weeks later or a service call 5 years later — our team is always available for you.
